Questions

What managers usually ask first.

What is included?

Every package coordinates the core experience and logistics. Inclusions vary by package and can cover the boat, venue, food, transport, facilitation and engagement records.

How many people can attend?

The standard packages are designed for teams of up to 12 or 15 people. Ask about larger groups during the discovery call.

Is this suitable for senior teams?

Yes. The Elite experience is built for senior teams requiring private transport, an executive venue and a fully managed day.

How do we start?

Start with a twenty minute call. We confirm the team, purpose and preferred timing, then check the required suppliers before offering dates.

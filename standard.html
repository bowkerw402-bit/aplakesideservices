The Standard experience pairs a GoBoat morning on Lake Burley Griffin with a catered session at East Hotel with AV included. It is the simplest route through the day: well organised, practical, and easy for the team to navigate.

What the day looks like

  • i.

    GoBoat on the lake

    Your team begins with a GoBoat experience on Lake Burley Griffin. The open setting creates the conversations and shared reference points that carry into the session.

  • ii.

    Walk to East Hotel

    The team makes its own way between the lake and East Hotel on foot. No shuttle or transfer is built into this tier.

  • iii.

    Catered session with AV

    East Hotel provides the meeting space, catering and AV. The facilitated session closes with practical commitments documented in a Team Operating Charter.

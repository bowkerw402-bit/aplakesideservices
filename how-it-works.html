How It Works

You make one call. We make the day.

The whole point is that this costs you almost nothing in time. Four steps, one of which is showing up.

01

A twenty minute discovery call

You tell us your team size, what you want the day to do, and roughly when. We talk through which experience fits — no scripts, no pressure. If it's not right for your team, we'll say so.

Your total planning effort: this call.

02

We arrange everything

Boats, catering, venue, facilitator — booked, confirmed and coordinated by us. We check live supplier availability before offering you dates, so every date we present is one we can actually deliver. You receive a single confirmed itinerary.

A 50% deposit confirms your date. The balance is due seven days before the event.

03

The day runs itself

We host from the jetty to the final session. Morning on the water, a proper catered lunch, and a facilitated afternoon built around how your team actually works. You participate like everyone else — you're not the organiser today.

04

Documentation, same day

That evening you receive the complete record: tax invoice, attendance register, facilitator summary of what the session covered and the commitments your team made, plus the full itinerary and supplier record. Clean paperwork, zero admin on your end.

Twenty minutes is all it takes to plan the whole day.
